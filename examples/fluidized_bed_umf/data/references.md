# External references

The acceptance reference and the measured closure are deliberately distinct.
Neither source is generated by, nor copied from, this repository.

- C. Y. Wen and Y. H. Yu, “A generalized method for predicting the minimum
  fluidization velocity,” *AIChE Journal* 12(3), 610–612 (1966).  The reported
  `Re_mf = sqrt(33.7^2 + 0.0408 Ar) - 33.7` correlation is the external
  acceptance reference in this example.
- I. F. MacDonald, M. S. El-Sayed, K. Mow, and F. A. L. Dullien, “Flow through
  porous media—the Ergun equation revisited,” *Industrial & Engineering
  Chemistry Fundamentals* 18(3), 199–208 (1979).  Its 180/1.8 packed-bed
  closure drives the live seam measurement.
- S. Ergun, “Fluid flow through packed columns,” *Chemical Engineering
  Progress* 48(2), 89–94 (1952).  Used only as the reported analytic bracket,
  not as the acceptance value.

The comparison is intentionally limited to these published correlations at the
declared air, particle, and voidage conditions.  It is not a substitute for a
separate CFD solver or laboratory replication.
